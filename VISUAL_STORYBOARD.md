## Animation Principles

1. **Smooth Easing:** All animations use ease-in-out
2. **Stagger:** Elements appear sequentially, not all at once
3. **Pulse Effects:** Used for emphasis (1.1-1.2x scale)
4. **Fade Transitions:** 0.5-0.8s between scenes
5. **Hold Times:** 2-3s for viewers to read text
6. **Flow Direction:** Left-to-right for natural reading
